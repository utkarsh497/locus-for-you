# Rahul Sharma

## Why this role

I've spent 6 years building backend systems that nobody owns end-to-end. At my current company, I design it, another team deploys it, another team monitors it. When it breaks at 3am, three Slack channels argue about whose problem it is. I want to own the whole thing — design to on-call. That's why this role exists in my head before I even saw this JD.

- Designed and shipped the payment reconciliation service at [Company] — processes $2M/day, 99.99% uptime for 18 months
